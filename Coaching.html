How we work

Training programs (customized and event specific options)

  • We build customized training programs to suit your specific needs, abilities, and time available so that you can reach your goals.
  • We create all of our custom training programs and event-specific training programs on Training Peaks using either heart rate zones or power zones.


What do you need:
  • GPS unit that is able to connect and upload to Training Peaks.
  • Heart rate monitor strap.
  • Open a free basic online account with Training Peaks.
  • Power meter - Optional.
